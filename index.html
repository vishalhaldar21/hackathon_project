Game Rules


  1. Guess a number between 1 and 50.
  2. You have 7 turns to guess the correct number.
  3. If your guess is too high or too low, the game will provide feedback.
  4. If you guess the correct number within 7 turns, you win!
  5. If you can't guess the correct number in 7 turns, the game will reveal the correct number.
